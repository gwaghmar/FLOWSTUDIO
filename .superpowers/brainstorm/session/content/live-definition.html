What does "Live" mean in your vision?

Defining the real-time nature of FlowStudio

A

Live Collaboration

Multiple users editing together in real-time. Shared cursors, instant updates, and collaborative brainstorming.

Live Data Integration

Diagrams connected to APIs or databases. Shapes change color or status based on real-world events or metrics.

Live AI Streaming

The AI draws and adjusts the diagram as you speak or type. No "Generate" button — just a continuous flow of creation.

The flowchart isn't just a picture; it's a working process. Deploy it as a workflow engine or a mini-app for employees.
