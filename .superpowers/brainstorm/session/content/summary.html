The FlowStudio Vision: "The Big Four"

A summary of the platform we are building

🤖

Fully AI Creation

Users type or talk; the AI builds the logical structure, selects the diagram type, and ensures WYSIWYG Aspect Ratio matches the target (Pitch Deck, LinkedIn, etc.).

✍️

Live Manual Editing

The "Power User" mode. Click and drag, edit JSON/Mermaid directly, or use a properties panel. The AI remains a "copilot" that helps clean up your manual changes.

🟢

Live Data & Execution

Flowcharts aren't static. They connect to APIs to show status (e.g., "Payment Received") and can "Run" actions (e.g., "Send Onboarding Email").

👥

Live Collaboration

A multiplayer workspace where teams and AI work on the same "live" business processes simultaneously.
